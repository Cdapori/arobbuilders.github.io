About us

House Construction Company.

AROB Builders is a locally owned construction and building company established in 2016 to help in the rebuilding of Bougainville.

AROB Builders Limited was established in 2016 with an objective of participating in rebuilding of Bougainville’s rundown infrastructure and at the same time create income earning avenue for the local populace. Most of these people are lost generation who lack basic education and who grown up to manhood during Bougainville’s civil unrest. Sighting that our Autonomous Bougainville Government (ABG) does not have enough money to train and equipped these populaces but have just enough to fund school classrooms and community Aid post AROB Builders was then established as a vehicle to participate in rebuilding of such infrastructure and at the same time train lost generation to be skilled human beings. Our company name was revived from AROB (Autonomous Region Of Bougainville) and Builders means to Rebuild Bougainville.
